## Usage tips
Choose a Genre from the 9 given. 
    
 <img width="1440" alt="Screen Shot 2022-02-15 at 6 43 52 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93455758/154168793-a45ebded-f78c-4a78-b51e-ab54bd823a72.png">
   
After choosing a Genre, choose one of the top 10 Artists of your choice. 
    
  <img width="1352" alt="Screen Shot 2022-02-15 at 6 45 39 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93455758/154168955-a6cb2f2a-783a-4909-93d1-e01fb30f4df2.png">
  
 After choosing an Artist. Choose a Track to listen to by that Artist. 
    
  <img width="578" alt="Screen Shot 2022-02-15 at 6 46 38 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93455758/154169049-3bbd98d8-cb15-4a6b-9043-ad299bcdd84e.png">
  
 Once you have chosen the track of your choice, you will be redirected to Spotify to listen to the track.
 
 <img width="1421" alt="Screen Shot 2022-02-15 at 6 47 49 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93455758/154169145-fc7e5b92-a313-4f11-8a43-aaff25803edc.png">
 
Want to check out the popularity of Top Track of your choice on Genre? 

Choose a Genre and scroll down to Top 10 Tracks: 
    
 <img width="1340" alt="Screen Shot 2022-02-15 at 6 49 07 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/93455758/154169267-d99f6eb3-6580-4e22-8abb-82c91fe6f783.png">
 
  Once you have selected the Genre of your choice, you will be able to see the Artist, Name of the Song, When it was released, and it's Popularity at the top.
